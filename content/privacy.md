_Last updated: May 14, 2026._

This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and what
your rights are. "We" and "our" refer to Jared Howard, operating through this
website and related applications.

## 1. What we collect

- **Account data** — name, email, and (where you create one) the credentials
  for accessing a software product we operate.
- **Billing data** — handled by Stripe. We receive the last four digits and
  brand of the payment method, billing country, and subscription state; we do
  not receive full card numbers or CVC.
- **Usage data** — server logs (IP address, request paths, timestamps,
  user-agent) used for debugging and abuse prevention.
- **Communications** — emails you send us, retained so we can follow up.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 2. Why we use it

- To provide and operate the services you've signed up for.
- To process payments via Stripe.
- To debug, secure, and improve the platform.
- To send service-related notifications (receipts, security alerts, material
  changes to these terms).
- To respond to support requests.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

## 3. Sharing

We share data only with sub-processors that help us run the service:

- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **GitHub Pages** — static hosting of this site.
- **Email provider** — for transactional and support email.

Where additional sub-processors are introduced (e.g. analytics, error
tracking), this page will be updated.

We may also disclose data when legally required (subpoena, court order).

## 4. Retention

Account and billing data is retained for as long as you have an active
relationship with us, and for a reasonable period afterwards to meet legal,
tax, and accounting obligations. Server logs are retained for up to 90 days.

## 5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct,
export, or delete personal data we hold about you, and to object to certain
processing. Email us at the address below to exercise any of these.

## 6. Security

We use industry-standard transport encryption (HTTPS) and rely on Stripe's
PCI-compliant infrastructure for payments. No system is perfectly secure, but
we take reasonable steps to protect data we hold.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the
current version; material changes will be notified to active customers.
