The best way to reach me is by email:

**[jaredhoward0912@gmail.com](mailto:jaredhoward0912@gmail.com)**

Use a subject line that matches your reason for writing — it routes faster
and gets a more useful reply:

- **Project enquiry.** Subject **"Project enquiry"**. Please include a
  short description of what you're trying to build, your rough timeline,
  and whether you already have a domain or hosting set up. I typically
  reply within a few business days.
- **Product support.** Subject **"Support — <product name>"**. Mention the
  product name and the email on your account so I can find your records
  quickly.
- **Cancel subscription.** Subject **"Cancel subscription"** plus the
  email address on your account. **Email cancellations take effect the
  day I receive them**; I'll confirm processing promptly. See the
  [Refund and Cancellation Policy](/refunds/) for full details.
- **Billing error or dispute.** Subject **"Billing error"** or **"Billing
  dispute"**. Include the charge date, amount, and account email. I aim
  to respond within a commercially reasonable time, typically a few
  business days — and most disputes resolve faster than a chargeback
  would.
- **Privacy request or appeal.** Subject **"Privacy request"** (or
  **"Privacy request — appeal"** if you are appealing a previously
  denied request). Tell me which right you want to exercise — know,
  correct, delete, portability, opt-out of sale/sharing, or appeal —
  and include the email associated with the personal information.
  Response times follow the timeframes required by the state-privacy
  law that applies to you, as described in the
  [Privacy Policy](/privacy/).

For anything else — partnerships, press, conference talks — write
whatever subject line makes sense and I'll get to it.
