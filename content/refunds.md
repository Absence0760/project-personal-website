## Subscriptions

You may cancel a software-product subscription at any time, either from your
account settings or by emailing us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of
the current billing period — you retain access until then, and you are not
charged for any subsequent period.

We do not pro-rate refunds for partial periods. If you believe you were
charged in error, or were billed after cancelling, email us within 30 days of
the charge and we will refund verifiable errors in full.

## Annual subscriptions

Annual plans may be cancelled at any time. Refunds for unused months on an
annual plan are considered on a case-by-case basis — email us with the
circumstances and we'll respond within 5 business days.

## Custom-development work

Refunds on custom-development projects are governed by the statement of work
signed for each engagement. By default:

- Deposits are non-refundable once work has commenced.
- Milestone payments cover work delivered up to that milestone and are
  non-refundable once accepted.
- Retainer fees cover availability for the relevant month and are
  non-refundable.

## Disputes

Before initiating a chargeback, please email us — most billing issues are
resolved within a day or two.
